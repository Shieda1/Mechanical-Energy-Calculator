What is the mechanical energy calculator?
The mechanical energy calculator is a tool that measures the energy which an object possess because of its position or motion.
How to calculate mechanical energy?
To calculate mechanical energy you should use the following formula:
mechanical energy = 0.5 * mass of an object * (velocity)^2 + mass of an object * 9.80665 * height from the ground
